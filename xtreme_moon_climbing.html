    • Xtreme Moon Climbing

    Scale some of the highest cliffs in the milky way

    Get the oppurtunity to see some of the most extraordinary views at the Moons of Mars resort. Our expert sherpas will take teams of 8 up Mount Deimos, the centerpiece of our resort. The total trip will go over two days, where you will be spending the night at the top. All equipment will be provided to you.

    Unlike a normal climb on earth, you will be able to climb much futher distances at much less effort, due the the weaker gravity on Deimos. Most climbers will describe this as one of their best experiences to date.

    * Participants are required to pass a fitness test prior to climbing.
